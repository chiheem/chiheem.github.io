About me

Hi! I am currently a Senior Research Scientist at Hitachi America, Ltd. I am also a Research Affiliate at the MIT Laboratory for Financial Engineering (LFE) and an incoming visiting scholar at the Stanford Computer Science Department.

My research focuses on developing artificial intelligence (A.I.) and data science tools, and applying them to a myraid of real-world problems in healthcare, finance, supply-chain, and other areas. My current projects involve developing new Graph Neural Network (GNN) architectures, and separately, creating and evaluating Large Language Model (LLM) agents.

I hold a B.Eng (First Class Honors) in Mechanical Engineering and a B.SocSci (First Class Honors) in Economics from the National University of Singapore, as well as an S.M. and Ph.D in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology.
